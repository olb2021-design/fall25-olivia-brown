Routine Reset Check-In

Objectives

  • Students reflect on classroom routines and identify what works and what doesn’t.
  • Students propose adjustments to improve fairness and efficiency in routines.
  • Students participate in discussion and vote on minor changes to the daily routine.

Materials

  • Whiteboard or chart paper
  • Markers
  • Routine reflection sheets (optional)
  • Voting cards or tokens

Steps

  1. Introduce the concept of a “Routine Reset Check-In” to the class and explain its purpose.
  2. Have students reflect on which classroom routines are working well and which need adjustment.
  3. Invite students to share their thoughts and discuss as a group, guiding the conversation to focus on fairness and community expectations.
  4. List proposed adjustments on the board and facilitate a class vote on small changes that can be implemented.
  5. Review agreed changes and set a plan to implement them for the next class session.
  6. Encourage students to revisit routines periodically to evaluate progress.
